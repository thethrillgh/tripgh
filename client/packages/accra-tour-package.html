ACCRA TOUR AND NIGHT LIFE: 10 DAYS 9 NIGHTS

TOUR HIGHLIGHTS (minimum group size of 10)
  • Welcome, assist and transfer to the hotel followed by a dinner, and after, a brief orientation on your tour.
  • A tour of the creative art industry in Accra, from museums, art galleries, wood cavers, bead makers, dress makers and more.
  • Relax in a serene environment at the Legon Botanical Gardens, and also visit Ghana’s Premium university.
  • Go on a hiking trail and game viewing at the Shai Hills Resource Reserve and interact with the inhabitants of Shai.
  • Relax under a hot African sun at La Pleasure Beach, and go for swimming in the sea.
  • Experience the amazing night life of the city.
  • A day and leisure in Accra.
  • Farewell dinner and departure.
